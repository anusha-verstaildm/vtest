Frequently Asked Questions

How does V-test health ensure good medical quality?

V-Test Health ensures good medical quality through a combination of highly qualified professionals, evidence-based medicine, stringent credentialing, quality assurance programs, advanced technology, compliance with regulatory standards, patient safety initiatives, and continuous education for staff.

Who is V-test, and how can  help me?

An V-test is like your good friend at the hospital who will guide you throughout your healthcare journey.

From registration and lab tests to discharge formalities, our V-test are well-trained to make you comfortable at the hospital.

He/She can help answer questions that you might fight difficult to ask the doctor or arrange for a follow-up consultation in case you need more clarity. If you aren’t satisfied with your first consultation, V-test will assist you with more relevant specialists to consult till you're satisfied.

Our V-test treat your health as a priority and do everything to make your hospital visit hassle-free!

Will V-test Health accept an outstation patient?

Yes, we welcome patients from all geographies. Just contact us with all your details and give us a call at +234567890 or visit our website, V-test.health, to ask any questions that you have.

Which all health insurance does V-test Health accept?

We accept all major insurance plans.

We have partnered with 50+ Insurance Service Providers & TPAs that can assist you financially with your treatment. If your policy allows for cashless treatments, our hospitals would also provide that.

We at V-test Health believe your treatment should be the only thing on your mind. Hence we have introduced a cardless, No-cost EMI facility for easy financing options. Please contact our support or speak to your V-test for more details.

What does V-test Health's Fixed Price Package include?

We commit the price of your treatment even before you get admitted! We have a strict 'transparent price range' policy wherein we promise 100% Fixed Price Packages. No hidden charges, no surprises, and no “out-of-pocket” expenses for all surgical procedures.
This typically includes:
Hospital admission charges
Surgery charges, including surgeon fees, anesthetist fees, and OT charges
Standard pre-surgery pathology and radiology diagnostic tests
Hospital stay
Cost of drugs consumed during a stay at the hospital
Medicines at discharge
Typical exclusions:
Cost of transportation to and from the hospital
Room upgrades
Cost of implants
These will be informed to you beforehand.

To know more, contact our support center, call us at +91 697xxxxx000, or visit our website, V-test.health, to get all your concerns addressed.

Does V-test Health provide any emergency surgical treatment?

Along with performing elective procedures, V-test Health handles all types of medical emergencies too.
During an emergency, timing is critical. Even a few seconds are valuable. We offer 24 X 7 ambulance services that can get you to the nearest V-test Health network hospital in the shortest time possible.
